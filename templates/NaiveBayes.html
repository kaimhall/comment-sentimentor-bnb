Comment Classifier


This is very quick-and-dirty prototype of text classifier app. App recognizes whether a comment posted on public discussion is considered insulting. Classifier uses TF-IDF technique (term frequency-inverse document frequency). Database (troll.csv, kaggle) has 3946 comments labeled neutral or insulting.

Model was built by extracting vocabulary into sparse matrix consisting of 3947 comments and 16469 different words. Bernoulli Naive Bayes classifier with a grid search was used to arrive at prediction. Score of the model is ~ 0.76. Most frequent words in comments have a high weight except if they appear in most of the comments (stop words).
